# Boundless Logos

Effect: Grants 1 extra level when choosing a Signet of Infinity. A special skill that creates an effigy to taunt enemies for 10s is available once Charge reaches 600. The puppet is Soul Linked to enemies so that 30% of the DMG it takes is passed on to all enemies, or 60% if there are more than 2 enemies on the field.
Charging: Gain 100 Charge when casting ELF Ultimate or summoning entities. CD: 4s.
