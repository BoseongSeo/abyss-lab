# Empty Dreams, Self, Set, and Joy

Reaching 500 Shiny Silvers boosts the effects of all Normal Signets of Reverie by 25%. Reaching 1000 boosts the effects by 50% and Physical and Elemental DMG by 60%.

## Improvised Drama
